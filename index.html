Who am I?

I was born in 1993 in Varna, Bulgaria. My father is a seaman, mother takes care of family bussiness and sister, well she is kind of a math prodigy. I grew up as a normal boy - with friends, outside, playing. After primary I went to the "German High School" - one of the better in my city. Just one year in and my parents decided I'm already old enough to start working for them - as a salesman.

This was not so bad. I earned my own buck and improved my communication skills at the same time. I kept this job for the next 8 years. After high-school I went to the "Marine Academy" where I got my Navigational-Engineering bachelor.

So, why did I start coding?

I've always liked computers and enjoyed math but life took me in another direction. After graduating the Maritime Academy I started working at sea. This gave me alot of free time. Having a pasion for learing I started reading on different topics.

Knowing my personality my Girlfriend and my Sister kept insisting I should try reading about Computer Science, saying I am going to love it. And as it turned out they were right - I loved it!

Ok, but what can I actually do?

Well, I'm fluent in English and German to start off with. I speak a bit russian as well, but just enough to make my russian grandmother proud. I can drive ships and sell just about anything.

And what about code?

You've guessed it. I am not much of an UI/UX guy. I rather enjoy building functionality. And if you have time to go trough my Projects you will see, that I have decent knowledge in:

  • Java Programming Language
  • Basic CSS, HTML and JS
  • OOP & Design, Algorithms, Data Structures & Basic Design Patters
  • Android Framework Components
  • Android Architecture Components (Room, LiveData, View-Model)
